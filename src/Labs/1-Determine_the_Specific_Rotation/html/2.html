Determining Optical Rotation

Using the Polarimeter we are changing angle to see at which angle light polarises.

1) Initially angle is set to 0 degree ...

2) First select the step size for the degree to increase or decrease from the options 1,5,10...

3) Than click on Plus or minus to increase or decrease the angle with the step size you have chosen...

4) See how the Intensity of the right part of light changes.

5)The moment when Intensity of the right part matches with the left part than the angle of Rotation you got is the Optical Rotation for that sample
