Measure ORD

Here we will measure for ORD for both sample we have seen.

1) Click on the "Swicth to ORD Mode" to enable the spectrometer to operate in ORD mode.

2) Select any sample by clicking on the corresponding button.

3) Click on the "Measure ORD" button to measure the ORD spectra of the selected sample.

4) Click on any other sample to measure the ORD spectra of that sample

5) Once you are done with the experiment click on "Analysing CD Spectra" to analysis page.
